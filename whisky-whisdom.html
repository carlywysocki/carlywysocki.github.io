Whisky Whidsoms

While at my internship at Kinetic Café, I worked with the graphic designers to help with a Facebook app for the company Canadian Club, called ‘Whisky Whisdoms’. The app was part of a contest where people could create their own pieces of wisdom, that could be shared and liked, to win a prize. Designs included a like page, gallery, share form, and image/comment page. I helped in putting together different aspects of the design, including the ‘Whisky Whisdom’ form, headers, the gallery, picture frame border, and like gate button.
